Cat's Cradle

Ellen Matthews

What is your style of improv?
Expressive. I find myself in everyday conversations using big physicality: I use my hands when I talk, I have intense facial expressions, and I get loud. This translates to how I improvise as well.

How do you describe your comedic voice?
Playful, joyful, committed, supportive, silly.

Some fun facts about yourself?
I love to dance (pop choreo preferred), lift weights, and run slowly.
