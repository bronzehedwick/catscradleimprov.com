Cat's Cradle

Nate Brown

I’ve been improvising for over twelve years in many forms and styles, including puppet improv, musical improv, and improvised one-act plays. I love to make connections and call-backs to previous scenes as a way of bringing the set together, and prefer scenes with more than two people, because “Hey! More people equals more friends!”

My comedic voice includes dad jokes, bad puns, taking everything literally, satirizing stupid rules and concepts, and taking about everything else besides whatever seems important.

I love watching characters get frustrated by never getting what they want. Also…ghosts, Draculas, werewolves, and frankensteins are hilarious. I can name all the states in alphabetical order and I’ve been poked in the eye by Ryan Seacrest.

I’ve been told recently that I’m good at puzzles.
